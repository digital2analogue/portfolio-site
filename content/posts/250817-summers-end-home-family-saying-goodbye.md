This week marks the end of a season of life for me. A season that started in February, beginning at the bottom of what has felt like an uncrossable, exhausting mountain. This month finally crested its peak. I said goodbye to my mom. I am closing chapters of life which are ready to be closed. There is more to go (there always is), but as they say, it is downhill from here.
